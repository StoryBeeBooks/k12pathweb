# K12Path Target Audience

## Primary Users
**Chinese immigrant parents** living in Western countries who want to:
1. Help their children **adapt to local Western education systems**
2. Guide their children to **excel academically** in their new country
3. **Navigate** the local school system (enrollment, curriculum, parent-teacher communication)
4. **Maintain Chinese language and cultural connection** while integrating
5. Find resources in **both Chinese and English**

## Key Resource Categories

### Essential (Western-focused)
- **Local curriculum apps** (Khan Academy, IXL, Prodigy, Epic!, etc.)
- **ESL/ELL resources** for language learners
- **Western school system guides** (how grades work, parent involvement)
- **Standardized test prep** (SAT, ACT, AP, A-Levels, IELTS, etc.)
- **College/university application guidance** for Western institutions
- **Social integration** resources (making friends, cultural adaptation)
- **Parent resources** in Chinese explaining Western education

### Optional (China-based)
- Chinese language learning apps
- Chinese curriculum resources (for maintaining heritage)
- Apps from China can be included but marked as supplementary

## Content Guidelines
1. **Primary language**: Chinese (for parents), with English resources for students
2. **Resource priority**: Western/international apps first, China-based apps as supplements
3. **Cultural sensitivity**: Help families balance integration with heritage preservation
4. **Practical focus**: Real resources that help navigate Western school systems
